Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our natural beef tallow lotion

What is beef tallow lotion?

Beef tallow lotion is a natural moisturizer made from rendered beef fat from grass-fed cows. It's rich in vitamins A, D, E, and K, and its fatty acid profile closely resembles that of human skin, making it exceptionally compatible with our skin's biology.

Why is beef tallow better than commercial lotions?

Beef tallow is rich in vitamins A, D, E, and K — all essential for skin health. It's incredibly nourishing, mimics our skin's natural oils, and helps lock in moisture without clogging pores. Because it's 100% natural, it's gentle on sensitive skin and free from harsh chemicals.

In contrast, many commercial lotions contain synthetic additives like parabens and phthalates. Parabens are preservatives that can disrupt hormones and may be linked to certain cancers. Phthalates are used to make scents last longer, but they're known endocrine disruptors and are banned in some countries.

Choosing beef tallow lotion means you're putting something on your skin that your body actually recognizes and can use — no toxins, no nonsense.

Is beef tallow lotion suitable for all skin types?

Yes! Beef tallow lotion is suitable for all skin types, including sensitive, dry, oily, and combination skin. Its natural composition makes it gentle and non-irritating, while its ability to balance skin's natural oils makes it effective for various skin concerns.

For dry skin, it provides deep hydration. For oily skin, it can help regulate sebum production. For sensitive skin, its natural ingredients are less likely to cause irritation compared to synthetic products.

What are the key benefits of using beef tallow lotion?
  • Deep Hydration: Tallow penetrates deeply into the skin, providing lasting moisture.
  • Vitamin-Rich: Contains natural vitamins A, D, E, and K that nourish and protect skin.
  • Anti-Inflammatory: Helps reduce redness and irritation.
  • Skin Barrier Support: Strengthens the skin's natural protective barrier.
  • Cellular Regeneration: Supports the skin's natural healing and renewal processes.
  • Non-Toxic: Free from harmful chemicals found in many commercial products.
Does beef tallow lotion have a strong smell?

Our pure beef tallow lotion has a mild, natural scent that most people find pleasant and subtle. It's not strong or overpowering. The natural scent typically dissipates shortly after application.

For those who are particularly sensitive to scents, the mild natural aroma of our tallow lotion is usually well-tolerated compared to products with artificial fragrances.

How often should I use beef tallow lotion?

Beef tallow lotion can be used daily, just like any moisturizer. Many customers apply it twice daily – once in the morning and once before bed. Because it's concentrated and effective, a little goes a long way.

For extremely dry skin or specific problem areas, you can apply it as needed throughout the day. The lotion absorbs well without leaving a greasy residue when applied in appropriate amounts.

Is your beef tallow sourced from grass-fed cows?

Yes, we exclusively use tallow from 100% grass-fed cows. Grass-fed tallow contains higher levels of vitamins and beneficial fatty acids compared to tallow from grain-fed cattle.

Our commitment to using only grass-fed tallow ensures that you're getting the highest quality, most nutrient-dense product possible for your skin.

Can I use beef tallow lotion on my face?

Absolutely! Beef tallow lotion is excellent for facial use. Its composition is similar to our skin's natural sebum, making it ideal for facial application. Many customers report improvements in various skin concerns when using tallow on their face, including reduction in fine lines, improved hydration, and calmer skin.

For facial application, we recommend using a small amount and gently massaging it into clean skin. It can be used under makeup and as part of both morning and evening skincare routines.

Is beef tallow lotion comedogenic (will it clog pores)?

Despite being derived from animal fat, properly rendered beef tallow has a low comedogenic rating and is unlikely to clog pores for most people. Its molecular structure is similar to human sebum, allowing it to absorb well without blocking pores.

Many customers with acne-prone skin report improvements in their skin condition when switching to tallow-based skincare, as it helps balance natural oil production without the pore-clogging ingredients found in many commercial products.

How long does a jar of beef tallow lotion last?

The shelf life of our beef tallow lotion is approximately 12 months when stored properly. To maximize shelf life, keep the jar in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

In terms of usage duration:

  • A 2oz jar typically lasts 1-2 months with daily facial use
  • A 4oz jar typically lasts 2-3 months with regular face and body use
  • An 8oz jar typically lasts 4-6 months for whole-body application

Since our lotion is concentrated and effective, a little goes a long way!

The Science Behind Tallow's Benefits

Biocompatible with Human Skin

The fatty acid profile of beef tallow is remarkably similar to the oils in human skin. This molecular similarity means your skin recognizes and can effectively utilize the nutrients in tallow, unlike many synthetic ingredients that merely sit on the surface.

Rich in Fat-Soluble Vitamins

Tallow contains natural, bioavailable forms of vitamins A, D, E, and K. Vitamin A promotes cell regeneration, vitamin D supports immune function in the skin, vitamin E provides antioxidant protection, and vitamin K helps with healing and reducing inflammation.

Strengthens Skin Barrier

The fatty acids in tallow help reinforce your skin's natural barrier function, which protects against environmental stressors, prevents moisture loss, and keeps irritants out. A healthy skin barrier is fundamental to overall skin health and appearance.

Deep, Non-Greasy Moisture

Tallow penetrates beyond the surface layers of skin to provide deep hydration without leaving a greasy residue. It helps balance your skin's natural oil production rather than simply coating the surface like petroleum-based moisturizers.

Soothes Irritation

The anti-inflammatory properties of tallow help calm irritated, red, or sensitive skin. Many customers report improvement in conditions like eczema, psoriasis, and dermatitis when switching to tallow-based skincare.

100% Natural Ingredients

Unlike commercial lotions that often contain dozens of synthetic ingredients, our tallow lotion contains only what your skin needs: grass-fed beef tallow and a small amount of organic olive oil. No preservatives, no fillers, no artificial fragrances.
