Frequently Asked Questions

What is beef tallow lotion?

Beef tallow lotion is a natural moisturizer made from rendered beef fat from grass-fed cows. It's rich in vitamins A, D, E, and K, and its fatty acid profile closely resembles that of human skin, making it exceptionally compatible with our skin's biology.

Why is beef tallow better than commercial lotions?

Beef tallow is rich in vitamins A, D, E, and K — all essential for skin health. It's incredibly nourishing, mimics our skin's natural oils, and helps lock in moisture without clogging pores. Because it's 100% natural, it's gentle on sensitive skin and free from harsh chemicals.

In contrast, many commercial lotions contain synthetic additives like parabens and phthalates that can disrupt hormones and cause irritation.

Is beef tallow lotion suitable for all skin types?

Yes! Beef tallow lotion is suitable for all skin types, including sensitive, dry, oily, and combination skin. Its natural composition makes it gentle and non-irritating, while its ability to balance skin's natural oils makes it effective for various skin concerns.

Can I use beef tallow lotion on my face?

Absolutely! Beef tallow lotion is excellent for facial use. Its composition is similar to our skin's natural sebum, making it ideal for facial application. Many customers report improvements in various skin concerns when using tallow on their face, including reduction in fine lines, improved hydration, and calmer skin.

How often should I use beef tallow lotion?

Beef tallow lotion can be used daily, just like any moisturizer. Many customers apply it twice daily – once in the morning and once before bed. Because it's concentrated and effective, a little goes a long way.

How long does a jar of beef tallow lotion last?

The shelf life is approximately 12 months when stored properly in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

In terms of usage duration:

  • A 2oz jar typically lasts 1-2 months with daily facial use
  • A 4oz jar typically lasts 2-3 months with regular face and body use
  • An 8oz jar typically lasts 4-6 months for whole-body application
