How to Use

Simple, Pure Ingredients

We believe in transparency and simplicity. Our tallow lotion contains only what your skin needs:

  • Grass-fed Beef Tallow: The main ingredient, rich in vitamins and nutrients that nourish your skin.
  • Organic Olive Oil: Adds smoothness and additional nutrients.
  • Essential Oils (Optional): For those who prefer a light, natural scent.

That's it. No fillers, no preservatives, no synthetic additives.

Why Tallow is Superior for Skin

Beef tallow has been used for centuries as a natural skin care solution, and for good reason:

  • Biocompatible: The fatty acid profile of tallow is remarkably similar to that of human skin, allowing it to absorb deeply and effectively.
  • Rich in Fat-Soluble Vitamins: Contains vitamins A, D, E, and K that support skin health and regeneration.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Helps calm irritated skin and may reduce redness and inflammation.
  • Deeply Moisturizing: Creates a protective barrier that locks in moisture without clogging pores.
  • Supports Skin Barrier: Helps maintain and repair your skin's natural protective barrier.

Application Tips

For best results:

  1. Start with clean, slightly damp skin for better absorption.
  2. Take a small amount of tallow lotion and warm it between your palms.
  3. Apply to your skin using gentle, circular motions.
  4. Allow a few minutes for complete absorption.
  5. For extremely dry areas, apply a slightly thicker layer before bed.

A little goes a long way! Our tallow lotion is concentrated and economical.
